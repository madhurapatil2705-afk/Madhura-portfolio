Groundwater
Quality Mapping

Spatial assessment of groundwater quality using Inverse Distance Weighted (IDW) interpolation in QGIS for groundwater quality assessment and resource management.

Introduction

Groundwater Quality Mapping was carried out using Inverse Distance Weighted (IDW) interpolation to convert groundwater quality measurements collected at sample locations into continuous spatial maps. The analysis helps identify areas with good and poor groundwater quality and supports groundwater resource planning, environmental monitoring and sustainable water management.

Study Area

Study Area : Sasewadi Village, Bhor Taluka, Pune District, Maharashtra, India

Objectives

  • Prepare groundwater quality maps.
  • Identify contamination hotspots.
  • Predict groundwater quality in unsampled areas.
  • Support groundwater resource management.
